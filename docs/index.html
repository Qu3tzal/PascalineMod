Introducing the "Pascaline" mod, a revolutionary addition to the world of Minecraft. Crafted for the Forge platform, Pascaline is not just another mod; it's an evolution, breathing life into the previously simple villagers of Minecraft. Drawing inspiration from the Pascaline mechanical computer devised by the legendary scientist Pascal, this mod integrates advanced artificial intelligence to villagers, enabling them to showcase behaviors, interactions, and responses that are unprecedented in the Minecraft universe. Step into a game where villagers are no longer mere bystanders but are dynamic entities, each with their unique personalities and roles, making your gameplay experience richer and more immersive than ever before.

Features:

  • Advanced AI Integration: Villagers now possess sophisticated AI, transforming their behaviors and interactions.
  • Dynamic Personalities: Every villager has its unique traits, ensuring no two villagers are the same.
  • Enhanced Villager Roles: From farmers to blacksmiths, each villager's profession is now more detailed and intricate.
  • Interactive Dialogues: Engage in meaningful conversations with villagers, unlocking new quests and stories.
  • Learning Mechanism: Villagers learn from player interactions, adapting and evolving their behaviors over time.
  • Village Evolution: Watch villages transform based on villager AI decisions, making each village unique.
  • Trade Revamp: Experience an improved trading system, with villagers offering diverse and dynamic trades.
  • Protection Mechanism: Villagers can now strategize and defend their village from threats more effectively.
